# TEKIMAX: Business Model Canvas

This Business Model Canvas (BMC) maps the strategic blueprint for TEKIMAX, aligning with the "Human-Centered AI" framework and the Lean Pitch Deck.

---

## 1. Customer Segments
*Who are we creating value for? Who are our most important customers?*
- **Primary (SaaS):** Incubators, Startup Accelerators, and University Entrepreneurship Programs.
- **Custom Software & Defense:** Government agencies and defense contractors requiring highly secure, isolated custom AI software architectures.
- **Consulting Clients:** Mid-market organizations seeking Digital Transformation, AI workflow audits, and Fractional CTO services.

## 2. Value Propositions
*What value are we delivering? Which customer problems are we solving?*
- **End-to-End Custom Engineering:** Designing, building, and deploying complex custom software systems with AI embedded at the core, built to scale and last.
- **Proof of Human Insight:** Cryptographically tracking organic strategy through the proprietary **Truthfulness Index** to ensure humans remain the pilot.
- **Provider Agnostic Infrastructure:** Instant access to 40+ models across Anthropic, Google, Mistral, and OpenAI without vendor lock-in.
- **Deploy in Minutes (Whitelabel):** Fully functional, white-labeled apps (like "Adaptive Startups") deployed immediately to custom domains with auth and billing built-in.
- **Strategic AI Consulting:** Expert guidance on dismantling lazy automation and structuring organizational workflows around Intelligence Augmentation.

## 3. Channels
*How do we reach our Customer Segments?*
- **Direct Sales (B2B):** Outbound sales connecting with C-Suites via the primary `tekimax.com` platform for high-ticket consulting and custom dev.
- **Self-Serve SaaS Portal:** The `customer-portal-provision` application allowing universities and incubators to spin up environments seamlessly.
- **Government & Defense Networks:** Participating in specialized contract bidding and defense tech cohorts.
- **Strategic Partnerships:** Co-marketing with compliance platforms (e.g., Vanta, Drata) targeting companies struggling with AI compliance.

## 4. Customer Relationships
*What type of relationship does each segment expect?*
- **High-Touch Onboarding:** White-glove setup for deploying the client's custom-branded dashboard and configuring their API access.
- **Platform-as-a-Service (Self-Serve):** For tech-savvy founders using the Provisioning Portal to spin up their own workspaces.
- **Strategic Auditing:** Ongoing quarterly check-ins providing clients with "Truthfulness Index" reports for their board and investors.

## 5. Revenue Streams
*For what value are customers willing to pay?*
- **Custom Software Contracts:** High-ticket, milestone-based revenue for building proprietary platforms and defense applications.
- **Consulting Retainers:** Monthly recurring revenue for Fractional CTO, Digital Transformation, and AI strategy auditing engagements.
- **White-Label Licensing Base Fee:** A recurring SaaS subscription ($X/month) for the dedicated infrastructure, custom branding, and RBAC portal.
- **AI Token Pass-Through + Margin:** Usage-based billing (negative churn model). Clients prepay for compute credits via Stripe, and TEKIMAX captures a margin on wholesale API rates.

## 6. Key Resources
*What critical assets are required to make the business model work?*
- **Intellectual Property:** The TEKIMAX "Truthfulness Index" telemetry and the AI Gateway Orchestration layer.
- **Human Capital:** Senior full-stack Rust/React engineers and protocol architects.
- **Infrastructure:** The Convex real-time database cluster and Cloudflare edge network.

## 7. Key Activities
*What are the most important actions to execute the value proposition?*
- **Platform Development:** Expanding the "AI Primitives" catalog and maintaining the `whitelabel` app templates.
- **Security & Compliance Check:** Continuous security auditing and penetration testing for strict data isolation.
- **Gateway load-balancing:** Managing API rate limits and routing enterprise traffic optimally across Google, Anthropic, and open-source models.

## 8. Key Partnerships
*Who are our key partners and suppliers?*
- **LLM Providers:** Anthropic, Google (Vertex), OpenAI, Mistral (for the underlying cognitive engines).
- **Infrastructure Partners:** Convex (backend-as-a-service), Cloudflare (hosting/edge), WorkOS (enterprise SSO auth), Stripe (metered billing).
- **Security Partners:** Firms validating the TEKIMAX architecture against strict industry security standards.

## 9. Cost Structure
*What are the most important costs inherent in our business model?*
- **Payroll (Licensing Developers):** Specialized engineering talent to maintain the high-performance architecture.
- **Assurance & Compliance:** Monthly/annual fees for continuous security monitoring platforms, penetration tests, and legal counsel.
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Monthly operational costs for Convex, Cloudflare Containers, and R2 storage.
- **API Token Working Capital:** Forward-purchasing volume tier LLM limits to ensure high availability during client usage spikes.
