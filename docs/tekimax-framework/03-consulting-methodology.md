# TEKIMAX Framework: Consulting Engagement Model

## Overview
TEKIMAX operates not just as a software vendor, but as a strategic partner. Our consulting arm teaches external organizations, agencies, and enterprise technical teams how to adopt AI responsibly using our **HCAI (Human-Centered AI)** methodology.

This document outlines our standardized lifecycle for consulting engagements.

---

## The Engagement Lifecycle

### Step 1: The "Pilot vs. Passenger" Audit
Every engagement begins with an organizational audit. We evaluate the client's current or proposed AI integrations and score them on the TEKIMAX **Truthfulness Index**.
- **Symptom Check:** Are employees delegating core strategic thinking to ChatGPT/Copilot? Are executives losing touch with the ground truth of their raw data?
- **Output:** A comprehensive risk report highlighting "Passenger Zones" where the company is outsourcing its unique value proposition to generic models.

### Step 2: HCAI Blueprinting
We design a custom architecture plan showing the client how to restructure their workflows.
- **Tooling Consolidation:** Replacing generic, unmonitored chatbot usage with purpose-built, sandboxed AI agents.
- **Friction Points:** Redesigning operational pipelines to ensure a human must explicitly review and sign off on automated tasks.
- **Metric Definitions:** Establishing the weights for their specific business (e.g., in a law firm, AI legal research might have a Micro weight, but AI-drafting the final brief has a Macro penalty if unsupervised).

### Step 3: Implementation & Training
TEKIMAX steps in to either build the software or train the client's internal engineering team to build it according to our structural standards (see `02-product-design.md`).
- **Socratic Training:** We train the workforce not just on *how* to prompt, but *why* they must maintain intellectual ownership of the outputs.
- **The "Force Multiplier" Paradigm:** We teach teams to treat AI like a hyper-competent junior analyst—capable of executing massive volumes of research, but requiring senior review.

### Step 4: The Executive Dashboard
We leave the client with an operational dashboard that continuously calculates their organization's Truthfulness Index. This provides the c-suite with real-time visibility into their culture's resilience against "lazy automation."

## Pedagogical Strategy (How We Teach HCAI)

When running workshops or seminars, TEKIMAX consultants employ the following talking points to secure buy-in:

1. **The Investor/Market Perspective:** "The market will soon price-in AI automation. Your company's valuation will not be based on how much AI you use, but on the proprietary human insight you possess that AI *cannot* replicate."
2. **The Security Perspective:** "Unmonitored AI reliance introduces massive shadow-IT risks. HCAI telemetry ensures you know exactly who is generating what."
3. **The Talent Perspective:** "If your juniors use AI to write everything, and your seniors use AI to read everything, your organizational IQ drops to zero. HCAI prevents talent rot."
